Have A Go!

Click anywhere on the canvas to add/remove live cells.

Click Start to begin animating each generation.

Click Stop to stop animating.

Click Randomize to generate live cells (1/3 chance a cell will be alive).

Click Clear to remove all live cells.

Use the slider to change the Speed.

Now Explore more patterns or go to the Conway LifeWiki to learn loads more!
